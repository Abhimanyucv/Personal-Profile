My Story

Hi, I’m Abhimanyu C V, an event management professional and the event enthusiast, passionate about turning moments into memories. With over three years of hands-on experience in the event industry, I specialize in planning, managing, and executing weddings, corporate functions, and cultural celebrations that leave a lasting impact.

In my career, I’ve led event operations and production for high-profile and large-scale gatherings — from government conclaves to luxury destination weddings. My professional journey includes working with Watermark Event Solutions and Carnival Group, giving me a unique balance of technical expertise (from my Computer Science background) and creative vision.

I believe in meticulous planning, seamless coordination, and crafting experiences that feel effortless for clients yet unforgettable for their guests. Whether it’s an intimate gathering or a grand celebration, my mission is to design and deliver events that tell a story, spark emotions, and exceed expectations.
